About Us

At our bakery, we're passionate about baking and creating delicious treats that bring joy to our customers. We started our bakery with a simple goal: to share our love of baking with the world. Our journey began two years ago when our founder, Rodelia H. Orca, also known as Mamada, started baking as a hobby during the pandemic. Over time, she developed her skills and began experimenting with different flavors and techniques. She started selling her baked goods at local markets and events, and the response was overwhelmingly positive. Encouraged by her success, she decided to continue baking, and our business bloomed. Today, we're proud to offer a wide variety of baked goods, from classic treats like chocolate chip cookies and cinnamon rolls to more unique offerings like matcha green tea cake and gluten-free options. But at our bakery, it's not just about the food. We believe that baking is a way to bring people together and create meaningful connections. That's why we're committed to using only the best ingredients and making everything from scratch. We want our customers to taste the love and care that goes into every single one of our products. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about us. We hope you'll come visit us soon and try some of our delicious baked goods.
